Breakfast Burrito

Home

Description

Filled with spicy sausage, smoky scrambled eggs, shredded cheese, and a bright, fresh avocado, these bodega-style breakfast burritos are delish any time of day!

Ingredients

  • 1 large avocado
  • 1 jalapeño pepper
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1 tablespoon lime or lemon juice
  • 1/2 lb spicy sausage (chorizo, Italian, etc.)
  • 1 1/3 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 4 10in flour tortillas
  • 4 large eggs
  • Vegetable oil, salt, pepper
  1. Scoop and smash the avocado in a bowl and mix along with the lime/lemon juice, diced red onion, diced jalapeño. Salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and set aside.
  3. Heat a pan over medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook, stirring frequently, until browned. Transfer the sausage to a plate and leave the dripping sin the pan.
  4. Add beaten eggs into pan with sausage drippings and cook on low heat. Salt and pepper to taste, and scramble the eggs. Once eggs are cooked, transfer to a plate and clean the pan.
  5. Open the tortilla, add about 1/4 cup of the avocado mixture onto each tortilla, followed by a quarter of the cooked sausage, quarter of the eggs, and 1/3 cup of cheese. Fold the tortilla into a burrito.
  6. Lightly oil the pan over medium heat and add the burritos, seams down. Cook until bottom of the burritos are golden brown, then flip the burrito until the other side is golden brown.
